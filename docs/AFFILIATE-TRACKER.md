## Safety reminders for future sessions

- Do not force affiliate links into articles where the program is not genuinely relevant
- Do not add more than one CTA block per article unless editorial context clearly supports it
- Do not imply paid tools are required when the article's subject is free alternatives
- Do not make endorsement or testing claims that are not backed by the article content
- Do not add CTAs to thin or newly published articles — wait until the page has editorial depth
- Keep `rel="sponsored noopener"` on all affiliate links
- Amazon links on software-only comparison articles are not permitted (hardware-adjacent only)
- Zoho links must not be placed until the CJ affiliate application is approved
