Professional video editing software costs hundreds per year. This section covers free alternatives for every video workflow: editing, screen recording, video calling, and content creation.

We test each tool for real tasks — cutting footage, recording a screen walkthrough, running a team video call — and document what the free tier actually covers before you run into a paywall or watermark.

That includes the limits people usually discover too late: export caps, time limits, watermark policies, codec support, and whether the software still feels usable once the trial-like perks are gone.
