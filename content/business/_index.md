Business software subscriptions can drain thousands per year from a small operation. This section covers genuinely free alternatives to the most common business tools: accounting, CRM, email marketing, invoicing, and project management.

We focus on the limits that change a buying decision in real life — client caps, invoice caps, user limits, automation restrictions, reporting depth, and the point where a paid tool starts saving more time than it costs.
